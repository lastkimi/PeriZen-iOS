"Don't disturb me, but let me know you're there."
PeriZen is a minimalist rebellion against digital noise. While traditional apps hijack your dopamine with pop-ups and gamified rewards, we advocate for profound Dopamine Desensitization. Visually almost invisible, it relies purely on faint ambient light and tactile haptics to keep you in an uninterrupted Deep Work Flow.

Core Features:
- Stacked Bloom Rendering: Simulates realistic multi-layer physical ambient light.
- Infinite Time Scroll: Break free from rigid time slots with absolute finger-swiping control.
- Gentle Posture Perception: No jarring alarms. When slouching is detected, subtle flashes and haptic ticks guide you back.
- Resonant Networking: Feel the presence of others through invisible dot arrays—companionable silence without the social pressure.
